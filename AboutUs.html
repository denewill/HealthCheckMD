HealthCheckMd

HealthCheckMd is a healthcare website where it can provide you with information regarding with different types of sickness and your current health status. The company was founded in 1989 by an American entrepreneur named John Helliot and it was originally a small clinic. Until the year 1997, the company decided to take its path online. When the company first started, it had only a few web visitors every week, now the company has over a hundred thousand visitors everyday and we seek to improve our service everyday to imrpove the quality of our service.
